Welcome to Infra

Infra exposes load balanacer. This may take a few minutes. To check if it's ready, run:

    kubectl get -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc  infra

Once ready, connect to infra via the exposed IP address or hostname:
    
    export SERVICE_IP=$(kubectl get svc -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} infra --template "{{"{{ range (index .status.loadBalancer.ingress 0) }}{{.}}{{ end }}"}}")
    infra login $SERVICE_IP
